Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with our product, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund. Simply contact our customer service team for assistance.

Is shipping free?

Yes, we offer free shipping on all orders within the continental United States. For international orders, shipping fees will apply based on your location.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping for an additional fee. You can select this option at checkout and include a personalized message with your gift.
